🎵 NJOY Music App

Welcome to NJOY, a modern music platform designed to bring music lovers, artists, and creators together in one place. NJOY is more than just a music player—it is a complete music experience where users can discover new songs, create playlists, upload music, connect with artists, and even explore live concert information. This project was created to provide an engaging, easy-to-use, and enjoyable platform for music enthusiasts while showcasing modern application development practices.

⸻

🌟 About NJOY

Music has become an essential part of our daily lives. Whether we are studying, working, traveling, or relaxing, music helps us stay motivated and entertained. NJOY was built with the vision of creating a simple yet powerful music application that allows users to enjoy their favorite tracks while also exploring new content from artists around the world.

The application focuses on user experience, performance, and accessibility. Every feature is designed to make music discovery and listening smooth and enjoyable. From creating personalized playlists to browsing artist profiles, NJOY aims to provide everything a music lover needs in one platform.

⸻

🏠 Home

The Home page serves as the central hub of the application. It is the first screen users see after logging in and provides quick access to the most important sections of the platform.

Features available on the Home page include:

* Trending songs and albums
* Recommended music based on user preferences
* Recently played tracks
* Popular artists
* Featured playlists
* Quick navigation to other sections

The goal of the Home page is to help users quickly find music they enjoy without having to search extensively.

⸻

🎶 Playlist

The Playlist feature allows users to organize songs according to their preferences.

With playlists, users can:

* Create custom playlists
* Add songs to existing playlists
* Remove unwanted tracks
* Rename playlists
* Organize music by mood, genre, or activity
* Access favorite collections anytime

Whether it is a workout playlist, study playlist, travel playlist, or relaxation playlist, users can personalize their listening experience exactly how they want.

⸻

🔍 Search

The Search feature helps users discover music quickly and efficiently.

Users can search for:

* Songs
* Artists
* Albums
* Playlists
* Genres

The search functionality is designed to deliver accurate and fast results, allowing users to find their desired content within seconds. This feature encourages music discovery and helps users explore new artists and tracks.

⸻

📤 Upload Song

The Upload Song feature is designed for artists and content creators who want to share their music with listeners.

Using this feature, artists can:

* Upload original songs
* Add song titles and descriptions
* Include album artwork
* Manage uploaded content
* Reach a wider audience

This section encourages creativity and allows independent musicians to showcase their work. Uploaded content can be organized and made available for listeners across the platform.

The NJOY Live Concerts section is an experimental feature currently developed for testing and demonstration purposes.

This section may provide:

* Upcoming concert listings
* Event dates and timings
* Artist performance schedules
* Venue information
* Ticket availability status
